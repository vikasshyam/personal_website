Enterprise Systems & Operations Advisory

Helping PE firms, mid-market companies, and enterprises reduce integration risk and consolidate systems.

What I Do

When companies merge, acquire, or reorganize, their systems rarely cooperate. HR platforms don't talk to payroll. Recruiting tools are fragmented across business units. Financial reporting breaks when org structures change. Someone needs to walk in, diagnose the mess, design the consolidation plan, and either execute it or hand it off with a clear playbook.

That's what I do. I've done it at a global automotive manufacturer, an international law firm, a Fortune 50 bank, and an independent medical practice. The scale changes — the methodology doesn't.

Who This Is For

  • Private Equity and Venture Capital firms conducting post-acquisition system audits and integration planning
  • Mid-market companies consolidating HR, payroll, or operational systems after M&A
  • Enterprises migrating to Workday or consolidating legacy HRIS/recruiting platforms
  • Expert network clients seeking technical-financial crossover expertise for due diligence
